The United States conducted visible air operations near Venezuela, a U.S. carrier strike group ordered toward the region remained stalled off Africa while reports said the White House was reconsidering strikes, and the U.S. Senate narrowly blocked a measure that would have restricted the president’s ability to strike Venezuela without congressional approval. Markets and sanctions-related actions also reacted to rising U.S. pressure, while Venezuelan forces and regional aircraft movements were reported amid heightened alert.
